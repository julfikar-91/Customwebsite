Our Academics

Comprehensive play-based to primary programmes for ages 3–10.

Cambridge Early Years Nursery

Age 3–4 years Mon–Fri

Child-centred and play-based. Young learners develop at their own pace, make choices, and build self-worth — getting the best start in life.

Cambridge Early Years Lower KG

Age 4–5 years Mon–Fri

Nurturing key milestones and foundational skills. Encourages curiosity, confidence, and early development to help children thrive both in school and everyday life.

Cambridge Primary Upper KG

Age 5–6 years Mon–Fri

A balanced curriculum with engaging resources, teacher training, and assessment tools. Builds strong early literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

Cambridge Primary Class I

Age 6–7 years Mon–Fri

Strengthens core academic concepts while nurturing creativity and values. Focuses on literacy, numeracy, and enquiry-based learning.

Cambridge Primary Class II

Age 7–8 years Mon–Fri

Expands learning through subject integration, hands-on projects, and collaboration. Supports holistic growth for intellectual and emotional development.

Cambridge Primary Class III

Age 8–9 years Mon–Fri

Introducing structured scientific inquiry and expanding social studies concepts. Encourages analytical thinking and deeper understanding of the world.

Cambridge Primary Class IV

Age 9–10 years Mon–Fri

Developing advanced analytical skills and linguistic competence. Prepares students for more complex academic challenges with confidence.
